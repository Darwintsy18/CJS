SPECIFIC CLASSIFICATION OF CRIME

Felony

-violation of revised penal code

EXAMPLE:

MURDER - with qualifying circumstances.

HOMICIDE - with no qualifying circumstamces.

PARRICIDE - direct blood relationship.

INFANTICIDE - less than 72 hours

Offence

- violation of special penal law

EXAMPLE: (VAWC) violence against womens and childrens.

Criminal

- is the main character of criminal justice system.

- (in criminal sense) a person who commited crime regardless whether reported or reffered.

- (in legal sense) a person who has found guilty beyond reasonable doubt.

Victim

- is a forgotten character of criminal justice system.
