Specific classification of crime

Posted on January 28, 2024 by Darwin

Felony

-> violation of revised penal code.

Example:

Murder -> with qualifying circumstances.

Including:

* Poison

* Fire

* Evident Premeditation

*Treachery

Homicide -> no qualifying circumstances.

Parricide -> killing direct blood relationship.

Infanticide -> killing a baby less than 72 hours.

Offence

-> violation of special penal law

Example: (VAWC) ->violence against womens and childrens.

Criminal

* (in criminal sense) -> a person committed crime regardless whether reported or reffered.

* (in legal sense) -> a person who has found guilty beyond reasonable doubt.

Victim

-> is a forgotten character of criminal justice system
